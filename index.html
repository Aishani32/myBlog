About

Dance allows people to be more active, socialize and develop creative and physical skills. Just a few of the benefits increased exercise dancing can give you are; reduced stress levels, improved relaxation, stronger bones & muscles, weight control and a healthier brain!My hobby is dancing, as it gives me joy and happiness.I have been learning Kathak for over a year now. Dancing allows me to convey anything I want, which there is no correct way to do so. There is always a story to tell with Kathak and that is the most interesting aspect.It helps me express my feelings and makes me feel free from all the duties and responsibilities. It is also a great exercise and helps me remain fit and flexible.Dancing requires a lot of patience and practice and though it can be repetitive at times, the end result is a great one! Dancing is my way to escape the cruelties of the world.
